OSAKA 5

The Osaka 5 refers to five pioneering Japanese denim brands—Studio D'Artisan, Denime, Evisu, Full Count, and Warehouse—that played a key role in establishing Japan as a leader in premium denim production. These brands are celebrated for their traditional craftsmanship, use of vintage shuttle looms, and dedication to creating high-quality, authentic selvedge denim.
