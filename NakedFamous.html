NAKED & FAMOUS

"Instead of relying on celebrity endorsements and expensive ad campaigns, we devote all of our resources to sourcing the world’s best fabric and turning it into awesome jeans."

Naked & Famous Denim is a Canadian raw denim brand founded by Brandon Svarc in 2008. The brand is renowned for its commitment to high-quality craftsmanship, unusual denim concepts, and minimalist, no-frills approach. Naked & Famous skips the embellishments typical of luxury brands and instead focuses on the fabric itself, which is sourced from some of the finest mills in Japan. Naked & Famous was created with the idea of rejecting the flashy marketing of other high-end brands. Svarc sought to create denim that was all about quality and innovation, without relying on celebrity endorsements or artificial distressing of the jeans. The brand name itself is a playful jab at the superficial world of fashion. Naked & Famous is celebrated for its wide range of innovative and experimental fabrics.
